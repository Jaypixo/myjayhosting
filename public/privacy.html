Privacy Policy

MyJay.net is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

By using MyJay.net, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Data Purpose
Email address Account creation, login, and account recovery.
Username Used to identify your account and create your public subdomain.
Password hash Stored in hashed form using PBKDF2-SHA256 with a unique salt. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
Site files and content Required to host and serve your website.
Profile information Optional information such as a site title or biography that you choose to publish.
Session cookie Keeps you signed in to your account.
Server logs May temporarily contain IP addresses and technical request information for security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the MyJay.net platform.
  • To authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • To host and publish user-created websites.
  • To investigate abuse, spam, security incidents, or violations of our Terms.
  • To communicate important service-related information.

What We Do Not Do

  • No third-party advertising networks.
  • No third-party analytics services.
  • No tracking pixels or cross-site tracking technologies.
  • No sale, rental, or brokerage of personal data.
  • No profiling of users for advertising purposes.

Basic page view counters may be provided for hosted sites. These are generated server-side and do not identify individual visitors.

User Content

Content you upload to MyJay.net is stored and served as part of the hosting service. Published websites are publicly accessible by design.

MyJay.net does not routinely review user content. However, administrators may access files when reasonably necessary to investigate abuse reports, security issues, legal complaints, or violations of our Terms.

Cookies

MyJay.net uses a single session cookie to authenticate logged-in users. This cookie is necessary for the service to function and cannot be used to track you across other websites.

The cookie is configured with appropriate security settings, including HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite=Lax.

Data Retention

Account information and hosted content are retained while your account remains active.

Server logs are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for operational, security, and troubleshooting purposes.

Account Deletion

When an account is deleted, the associated account record, hosted files, and published website content are permanently removed from the platform.

Some technical records, such as temporary server logs, may persist for a limited period before automatic deletion.

Account deletion is currently performed by an administrator. A self-service deletion feature may be added in the future.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your account.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be announced through the platform or development updates.
